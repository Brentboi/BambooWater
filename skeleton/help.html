What is Bamboo Water?

Bamboo Water is a web application that allows the user to record his/her fiber and water intake for the day.

Adding an Entry

  1. To add an entry into the log you would press the Add button, which causes a window to popup.
  2. You can choose to add food or water.
  3. Then you can type the name of the food followed by amount and finally units (grams or liters).
  4. After, you can press the Add button on the popup which will add the entry to the log and close the popup.
  5. The new entry should appear at the top of the log.

Editing an Entry

  1. To edit an entry on today's log you need to click on the entry.
  2. Once that happens a window should pop up with the details of that entry where you can edit the details of that entry.
  3. You can then either click Update button which will update the entry with the new details you have added, or you can press the Delete button which will remove the entry from the list.
  4. The entry should be modified.

Viewing Log of a Previous Day

  1. To view a log of a previous day, all you need to do is go to the history page by pressing the History button on the menu bar at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Once there you should see a list with different dates and the amount of fiber and water consumed each day.
